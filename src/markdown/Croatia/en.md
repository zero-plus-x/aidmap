### Free travel options

People fleeing from Ukraine can use [HŽ Putnički prijevoz](http://www.hzpp.hr/hrvatska-za-ukrajinu?p=271) trains free of charge with ‘solidarity tickets’ and with a “Help Ukraine Ticket”.
